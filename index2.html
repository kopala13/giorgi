Dinosaurs

Facts about Dinosaurs

Dinosaurs are a diverse group of reptiles that once roamed the Earth millions of years ago. They first appeared during the Mesozoic Era, which is often referred to as the "Age of Dinosaurs."

Types of Dinosaurs

There were many different types of dinosaurs, ranging from the massive and fearsome Tyrannosaurus Rex to the gentle and herbivorous Brachiosaurus. Dinosaurs came in various shapes and sizes, and they inhabited different environments, from lush forests to open plains.

Extinction

Unfortunately, all non-avian dinosaurs went extinct around 65 million years ago, likely due to a catastrophic event, possibly a massive asteroid impact. However, some of their descendants, modern birds, still thrive today.

Discovering Dinosaurs

Paleontologists study fossils and rock layers to learn more about dinosaurs and their prehistoric world. Over the years, many fascinating dinosaur fossils have been unearthed, shedding light on these incredible creatures that once ruled the Earth. All About Dinosaurs
